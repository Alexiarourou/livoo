Everything you need to know

Clear answers about housing, flatmates, community and how Livvo works, with no small print.

Livvo is a housing and community platform built for students, interns, young professionals and digital nomads moving abroad. We offer verified furnished rooms and flatshares for 1–12 months, a flatmate matching tool, and local community groups in each city, all in one place. No guarantor required, no long leases, no endless paperwork.
Anyone moving to a new city for a few months to a year. Erasmus and exchange students, interns, young professionals on placement, remote workers and digital nomads. If you need flexible housing and want to arrive somewhere with people already around you, Livvo is for you.
No. All Livvo landlords are briefed to work with international tenants who cannot provide a local guarantor. Instead, you verify your stay through your university acceptance letter, student ID, or work placement confirmation. Simple, fair and designed for people on the move.
Stays on Livvo range from 1 to 12 months. Each landlord sets their own minimum stay, which is clearly listed on every property. All duration terms are agreed directly between you and the landlord. Livvo makes the connection and keeps things transparent.
A flatshare means renting a private room in a shared apartment with other tenants. It is a great option if you want company and lower costs. A rental is a full apartment or studio for sole use. It is better if you want privacy. Livvo lists both. Use the filters to find what suits you.
Create a free profile with your budget, lifestyle, move-in date and personality. Livvo suggests compatible matches based on your preferences. You can message potential flatmates before you arrive, so you're not meeting strangers on move-in day. It's like dating apps, but for housemates.
