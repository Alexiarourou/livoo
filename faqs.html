Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Livvo — flatshares, community, and moving abroad.

Livvo is a platform for students, interns and young professionals moving abroad. We help you find flatshares and furnished rentals for 1–12 months — plus a community to connect with flatmates and locals before you arrive. No guarantor, no long lease, no impossible paperwork.
Erasmus students, exchange students, interns, young professionals on placement, and digital nomads. Anyone moving to a new city for a few months to a year who needs flexible housing and wants to find their people before they land.
No. Livvo landlords understand that students and interns often can't provide a local guarantor. You verify through your university email or work placement letter instead. We make it simple so you can focus on your experience abroad.
Each landlord sets their own minimum and maximum stay. You can find properties for 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, 9 months, 12 months or more. Duration is always agreed directly between you and the landlord — Livvo just makes the connection.
A flatshare means you rent a room in a shared apartment with other tenants. A rental can be a whole apartment, studio or house. On Livvo you'll find both — filter by "Flatshare" if you want to live with others, or browse studios and apartments for more privacy.
Yes. The Livvo community is free for everyone — whether or not you rent through us. Create a profile, join student groups, find flatmates, get city tips and meet people. You don't need to book a property to be part of the community.

Create a landlord account, verify your identity, then add your property with photos, amenities, price and duration. You'll reach thousands of vetted students, interns and nomads. You set the terms — Livvo handles the connection.
Browsing and joining the community is free. There are no hidden fees to create an account or search for properties. Any fees for bookings or listings are clearly communicated before you commit.
For general questions, email us at hello@livvo.net. For landlords, you can sign up at the List Property page. We're building Livvo for people like you — we'd love to hear from you.
