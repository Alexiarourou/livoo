About Us

Livvo helps students, interns and young professionals find a home and their people when moving abroad. No guarantor, no hassle — just a smoother start to your adventure.

Our Mission

Moving to a new city is exciting — but it's also scary. Finding a place to live, a flatmate you gel with, and a community that gets you shouldn't feel impossible. Livvo exists to make that easier.

We offer verified flatshares and furnished rentals for 1–12 months, plus tools to match with compatible flatmates before you land. Our goal is simple: help you find a home and find your people.

Who We Are

Livvo was built for people who've been through it — the stress of flat hunting abroad, the joy of meeting the right roommate, the loneliness of arriving somewhere new without a network. We're here to change that experience for the better.

What We Offer

Flatshares & rentals — Furnished rooms for 1–12 months in European cities like Athens, Paris, London, Barcelona and more. No guarantor required — we verify you through your university or work.

Find a flatmate — Match with people based on budget, lifestyle, nationality and move-in date. Connect before you arrive so you're not starting from zero.

Community — Student groups, city guides, events. Because a city feels different when you know people.
