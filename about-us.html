We exist because moving abroad is hard enough.

Livvo brings together verified housing, flatmate matching and local community, so your next chapter starts with a home and people in it.

Our Mission

Moving to a new city should feel like an adventure, not a nightmare. Finding a place you trust, a flatmate you actually like, and people who get you. That shouldn't take months of stress and scam listings. We built Livvo to make that first step feel human again.

Who We Are

Livvo was built by people who've lived it: the exhausting flat search, the awkward flatmate mismatches, the loneliness of arriving somewhere new with no network. We're not a listing aggregator. We're a community-first platform that happens to have great housing.

I started Livvo after watching too many friends arrive abroad lost: no flat, no people, no idea where to start. I wanted to build the thing I wished existed. A place where finding a home and finding your people happen together. That's Livvo.

What Livvo gives you

Verified rooms & flatshares: Furnished, flexible stays from 1–12 months in Athens, Paris and London. No guarantor required.

Flatmate matching: Meet compatible housemates before you arrive, based on lifestyle, budget and move-in date. No more strangers.

Local community: City groups, events and real connections. Because a city feels completely different when you know people.
