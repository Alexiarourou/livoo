Sounio is stunning — the temple at sunset is worth it. Beach is ok, a bit rocky. For proper swimming I'd recommend taking the ferry from Rafina to Andros or Evia. Rafina port is 45 min by bus from Athens. Hydra is also doable as a day trip if you leave early.
